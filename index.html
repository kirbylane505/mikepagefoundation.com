About the Festival

Saturday, September 5th, 2026 | Festival Park, Elgin IL

The Love on the Lawn Music Festival (LOTL), presented by Impossible Dreamz Music Group and the Mike Page Foundation, is Elgin's annual "family reunion" on Festival Park's beautiful grounds.

Held every Labor Day Weekend, the festival brings together thousands of people for a full day of music, community, and celebration. LOTL has become a cornerstone community event—recognized by the City of Elgin and honored by Mayor Dave Kaptain with an official proclamation of "Love on the Lawn Day."

The Mike Page Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to neighborhood improvement, family support, civic engagement, and community wellness through music, youth development, and quality entertainment in a family-friendly environment.

Our Programs

Creating opportunities through sports, music, and community care

🎪

Love on the Lawn Festival

Our flagship annual community festival at Festival Park in Elgin. Over 20,000 cumulative attendees across 4 years, zero safety incidents. Mayor Kaptain declared Love on the Lawn Day an official City of Elgin holiday. Year 5 is September 5, 2026.

🏈

Youth Sports Sponsorship

We proudly sponsor the Elgin Northwest Tide, Elgin Steelers youth football teams, and Elgin Fire Soccer team, giving young athletes the support they need to compete and grow.

🎵

Music & Arts Initiative

Through our connection with Impossible Dreamz Music Group and MyStation, we create pathways for young artists to develop their talents and pursue careers in the music industry.

🍽️

Feed Our Friends

Meal distribution program that has served over 200 families in Elgin. Runs through our bilingual restaurant partner network to reach Spanish-speaking families where they actually are.

🚨

Emergency Response

When the December 2024 Elgin Tent City fires displaced 100+ families, we had meals, clothing, and volunteers on the ground inside 24 hours. When you see people hurting, you help.

🎓

Youth Builder Scholarship + Builder's Stage

New for 2026. Four $2,500 scholarships per year awarded to Elgin-area kids who demo real AI and tech projects on the Builder's Stage at Love on the Lawn before a 10,000-person audience. MPF scholarship starts with the youth.

👕

Clothing Drives

Coats, shoes, pants, and blankets every season for Elgin families. Distributed through our Spanish-language partner restaurant network to reach the 45.7% Hispanic community.

💻

Technology & AI

MPF's technology pillar including MyStation streaming platform, VPOD AI business plan generator, and our K-8 AI education partnership with Buildrs Nation. Kids in Elgin learn AI from mentors who ship real products.

Our Partners

Mission partners and programmatic allies powering MPF's work

🧠

Buildrs Nation

Minnesota 501(c)(3) (EIN 41-5061913) founded by Craig Clay, a self-taught technologist from North Minneapolis who built the Omo Quest learning platform solo. Free mentor-led AI education for K-8 youth. Three tracks: AI Explorers (K-2), AI Creators (3-5), AI Builders (6-8). Partnership announced April 19, 2026. MPF's K-8 AI education curriculum partner.

buildrsnation.org

🎤

Impossible Dreamz Music Group (IDMG)

MPF's sister music label and publishing platform. Working-pro instruction for kids in production, songwriting, and performance. Co-founded by Tyrell Thornton (100M+ career streams, BET+ "I Got a Story to Tell" Season 2).

idmglive.com

📡

MyStation

MPF's streaming and creator platform where young people record, publish, and learn from pros who've actually charted. Part of the music education pipeline.

mystationlive.com

🏀

QuickRun

USPTO-registered basketball platform with 64,000+ courts mapped across 5 U.S. cities. Target: 25,000 paid youth jobs through its Ambassador program. Pay kids to officiate games and maintain courts in their own neighborhoods.

quickrunlive.com

⭐

Ricky Davis Legacy Foundation

Youth and community programs including Feed Your City Challenge and Dribble Out Hunger. Founded by NBA veteran Ricky Davis. LOTL partnerships and business development lead Edward "EB" Davis.

rickydavislegacyfoundation.org

🌯

Spanish-Language Partner Restaurants

Ten local Elgin restaurants and bakeries power our bilingual outreach and food distribution: El Faro, El Paraiso, El Patio, Los Comales, Taqueria Chapala, Coronado Mexican Kitchen, Del Carmen Bakery, El Chicanito Bakery, Pastigel Bakery, Supermercado La Salsa. Coordinated by Kathy Rivas.
