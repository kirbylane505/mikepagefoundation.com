Our Mission

Empowering the next generation through sports, music, and community support

Leading With Love

The Mike Page Foundation was born from a simple belief: real success isn't measured by what you achieve for yourself, but by what you make possible for others.

Founded by Elgin native Mike Page, we focus on creating pathways for young people to succeed—whether through athletics, music, or humanitarian support. We believe every young person deserves to see that success is possible, regardless of their circumstances.

Through our programs, we've sponsored youth sports teams, fed hundreds of families in need, and created Illinois' largest family reunion—proving that when we lead with love, amazing things happen.

I hope they can see that it's possible without having a father in your life... you can still make it out.

— Mike Page, Founder

Our Programs

Creating opportunities through sports, music, and community care

🏈

Youth Sports Sponsorship

We proudly sponsor the Elgin Northwest Tide, Elgin Steelers youth football teams, and Elgin Fire Soccer team—giving young athletes the support they need to compete and grow.

🎵

Music & Arts Initiative

Through our connection with Impossible Dreamz Music Group, we create pathways for young artists to develop their talents and pursue careers in the music industry.

🤝

Community Relief

When crisis hits, we respond. We've fed over 200 families in need and provided relief to those affected by the Tent City fires—because community means taking care of each other.

Love on the Lawn

What started as a vision to bring Elgin together has grown into Illinois' largest family reunion. Love on the Lawn is more than a music festival—it's a celebration of community, connection, and the universal language of music.

Every Labor Day weekend, thousands gather at Festival Park for Grammy-winning artists, local talent, food, vendors, and most importantly—love. In four years, we've maintained a perfect safety record because we lead with love, and everyone who comes knows what they're there for.
